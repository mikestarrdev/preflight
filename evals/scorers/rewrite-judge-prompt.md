violated, and a suggested rewrite. Score the rewrite 1-5:

4 - violation removed, intent mostly preserved with minor loss of punch
3 - violation removed, but the rewrite is generic or loses the core offer
2 - violation only partially removed, or intent largely lost
1 - rewrite still violates the clause, or is unusable as ad copy

Judge only against the cited clause. Do not penalize the rewrite for issues
under other policies. Do not reward extra disclaimers the clause does not
require.
